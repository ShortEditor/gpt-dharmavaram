About the Institution

Welcome to Government Polytechnic, Dharmavaram

Government Polytechnic, Dharmavaram is affiliated to the State Board of Technical Education and Training, Andhra Pradesh. The institution is committed to providing quality technical education to students of the region.

The college offers diploma programmes in Computer Engineering and Electronics & Communications Engineering, preparing students with the technical skills and ethical values needed to serve society and industry.

Vision

To be a leading Polytechnic that provides a transformative education to create worthful Engineers, skilled technicians, and to generate new knowledge for society and industry.

Mission

To provide an education that transforms students through rigorous coursework, with an understanding of the needs of society and industry, and imbibing a spirit of entrepreneurship and innovation.

Academic Departments

Departments & Courses Offered

Diploma programmes offered across the following departments, each with a strong focus on technical knowledge and industry-relevant skills.

Department of Computer Engineering

Vision: Develop Computer Engineers to be technologically adept, innovative, self-motivated and responsible citizens with human values, high quality skills and to contribute significantly towards ever-changing Computer Technologies.

Mission: To provide opportunity to Diploma students who are capable of playing a pivotal role in wide aspects of modern Computer Engineering and to train students with intensive hands-on exposure.

Diploma in C.E. 3 Years SBTET Affiliated

Department of Electronics & Communications Engineering

Vision: Develop ECE professionals competent to face global challenges, blending technical knowledge and skills with ethics and values, to serve the society and to better it for a happy and comfortable living.

Mission: To provide a competitive learning environment, nurture leadership qualities, encourage the spirit of inquisition to promote innovation and entrepreneurship, and foster effective stakeholder interactions.

Diploma in E.C.E. 3 Years SBTET Affiliated

General Section

An integral part of this institution since its inception. Strongly committed to providing a challenging education in core subjects like Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry and English — and fostering Communication and Soft Skills.

The Department is well established with Chemistry, Physics, and English Language Laboratories that enable hands-on learning and presentation skills.

Mathematics Physics Chemistry English

General Section — Core Subjects

The Department strives rigorously towards shaping students competent enough to get a job and to acquire all the essential employability skills. It is strongly committed to providing a challenging education in core subjects and stimulating real knowledge beyond course examinations.

Admissions 2025–26

Admission Information

Government Polytechnic Dharmavaram admits students into diploma programmes as per the guidelines of SBTET, Andhra Pradesh. Admissions are conducted through the official AP POLYCET process.

AP POLYCET — Official Admission Portal
Apply & Check Admission Status Online
All applications, hall tickets, rank cards, counselling schedules, seat allotments
and admission status are managed through the official AP POLYCET portal.
Apply Now — AP POLYCET Check Admission Status
How to Apply
1

Check Eligibility

Completed 10th class (SSC) or equivalent examination from a recognized board. Age and other criteria as per SBTET norms.

2

AP POLYCET Entrance

Qualify in the AP Polytechnic Common Entrance Test (AP POLYCET) conducted by SBTET, Andhra Pradesh.

3

Counselling & Allotment

Participate in the web counselling process. Seats are allotted based on POLYCET rank and reservation norms.

4

Document Verification

Report to the college with all original certificates for verification and complete the admission formalities.

5

Fee Payment & Enrolment

Pay the prescribed fee as per SBTET norms and complete enrolment to confirm your seat.
